About Fantasy Physics


What is "Fantasy Physics"?

Fantasy Physics is a game in which the players serve as "The Executive" of a crew of physics experiments. The Executives create their crew by participating in a draft in which all crew members are physics experiments that are actually conducted.

As an interactive game, Fantasy Physics promotes learning and entertainment by combining the elements of one of the largest official physics conferences in the world and online gameplay. It is for all physics lovers and is a great way to get individuals interested in physics.



Why Physics?

Physics is the study of matter, energy, and everything in between. It is the base for biology, chemistry, engineering, astronomy, and so much more.

The studies and experiments orchestrated within the field, from galaxy clusters, to technological advancements, to natural phenomena, explain the universe we live in, which is vital to the continued, and future, progress of humanity.
