About Fantasy Physics


What is "Fantasy Physics"?

Fantasy Physics is an online contest that orients players against each other in an intellectual battle the likes of which this world has never seen. Fantasy Physics in a fun game for any and all physics lovers. Fantasy Physics promotes learning and entertainment by combining the elements of one of the largest official physics conferences in the world and competition.



Why Physics?

Physics encompasses more than just the study of how an apple falls from a tree or the way a rocket is propelled. The study of physics has applications from the largest galaxies to the smallest subatomic particles. Physics is an underlying factor of many other sciences, including chemistry, seismology, and astronomy.
